The Yaht-Tac Game

Take another look at the well-known game of Tic-Tac-Toe.

Get a second chance right before the fun is over.

Start a normal game on the board 3 by 3 cells. Whenever you feel squeezed on the board, roll the dice.

The game is now extended to the board of 5 by 5 cells.

Upon rolling the dice, make your move. The winning combination is now "four-in-the-row".

It does make a difference...
